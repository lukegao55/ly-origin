Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

RoundBuddy (“the app”) is a bag-work and combat-sport training app. We respect your privacy and keep the app simple: the app does not require an account and does not intentionally collect personal information.

Information We Collect

RoundBuddy does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account credentials. Training history, check-ins, plan progress, and language preferences are stored locally on your device.

Local Data

The app may save local training data such as completed rounds, session duration, combo counts, and plan progress. This data stays on your device unless you choose to remove the app or clear your records inside the app.

Third-Party Services

RoundBuddy uses Apple system features such as text-to-speech and in-app rating prompts. We do not add third-party advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs.

Children’s Privacy

RoundBuddy is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at luke@ly-origin.com.

隐私政策

更新日期：2026 年 6 月 13 日

我们收集的信息

拳伴不要求注册账号，也不会主动收集姓名、邮箱、手机号或账号凭据。训练记录、打卡记录、计划进度和语言偏好会保存在你的设备本地。

本地数据

App 可能会在本机保存已完成回合、训练时长、连招数量和计划进度等数据。除非你删除 App 或在 App 内清空记录，这些数据不会离开你的设备。

第三方服务

拳伴会使用 Apple 系统能力，例如语音朗读和评分提示。我们没有接入第三方广告 SDK，也没有接入第三方分析 SDK。

联系我们

如果你对本隐私政策有任何问题，可以通过 luke@ly-origin.com 联系我们。
