Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

CrazyFam (“the app”) is a casual party game. We respect your privacy and keep the app simple: the app does not require an account and does not intentionally collect personal information.

Information We Collect

CrazyFam does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, account credentials, contacts, photos, precise location, or personal messages. Game settings, language preferences, sound settings, unlocked content, and local gameplay statistics may be stored on your device.

Local Data

The app may save local data needed for gameplay, such as preferences, selected themes, voice settings, and game statistics. This data stays on your device unless you delete the app or clear related app data through your device settings.

Purchases

If the app offers in-app purchases, purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card information.

Third-Party Services

CrazyFam uses Apple system services such as App Store purchases and rating prompts. We do not add third-party advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs.

Children’s Privacy

CrazyFam is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at lukegao55@gmail.com.
