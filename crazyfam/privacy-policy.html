Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

CrazyFam ("the app") is a casual party game. We respect your privacy and keep the app simple: the app does not require an account, does not show third-party ads, and does not intentionally collect personal information from users.

Information We Collect

CrazyFam does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, account credentials, contacts, photos, precise location, personal messages, or other information that directly identifies you.

The app may store gameplay-related information locally on your device, such as language preferences, sound settings, selected themes, unlocked content, and local gameplay statistics. This information is used only to provide and improve the in-app experience on your device.

Local Data

Local data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers. You can remove this data by deleting the app or clearing related app data through your device settings, where supported by the operating system.

Device Permissions

CrazyFam does not request access to sensitive device data such as contacts, photos, precise location, camera, or microphone unless a future feature clearly requires it. If a future version adds a permission-based feature, the app will request permission through the operating system before using that feature.

Purchases

If the app offers in-app purchases, purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card information. Apple may process purchase, refund, subscription, tax, fraud prevention, and billing data according to Apple's own policies.

Third-Party Services

CrazyFam may use Apple system services such as App Store purchases, restore purchases, and rating prompts. We do not add third-party advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs.

Children's Privacy

CrazyFam is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review and address the issue.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when the app changes or when legal, technical, or operational requirements change. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at lukegao55@gmail.com.

隐私政策

更新日期：2026 年 6 月 13 日

CrazyFam（以下简称“本 App”）是一款休闲聚会游戏。我们尊重你的隐私，并尽量保持产品简单：本 App 不要求注册账号，不展示第三方广告，也不会主动收集可识别个人身份的信息。

我们收集的信息

CrazyFam 不会收集姓名、邮箱地址、手机号码、账号凭据、通讯录、照片、精确位置、个人消息，或其他可直接识别你身份的信息。

本 App 可能会在你的设备本地保存与游戏体验相关的数据，例如语言偏好、音效设置、已选择的主题、已解锁内容和本地游戏统计。这些信息仅用于在你的设备上提供和改善 App 内体验。

本地数据

本地数据会保留在你的设备上，不会发送到我们的服务器。你可以通过删除 App，或在操作系统支持的情况下通过设备设置清除相关 App 数据来移除这些数据。

设备权限

CrazyFam 不会请求访问通讯录、照片、精确位置、相机、麦克风等敏感设备数据，除非未来某项功能明确需要。如果后续版本新增需要权限的功能，App 会在使用该功能前通过操作系统请求你的授权。

购买

如果本 App 提供应用内购买，购买流程将由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理。我们不会接收或保存你的银行卡信息。Apple 可能会根据其自身政策处理购买、退款、订阅、税务、反欺诈和账单相关数据。

第三方服务

CrazyFam 可能会使用 Apple 系统服务，例如 App Store 购买、恢复购买和评分提示。我们没有接入第三方广告 SDK，也没有接入第三方分析 SDK。

儿童隐私

CrazyFam 不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。如果你认为儿童通过本 App 提供了个人信息，请联系我们，我们会进行核查并处理。

政策变更

当 App 功能变化，或法律、技术、运营要求发生变化时，我们可能会更新本隐私政策。任何更新都会发布在本页面，并更新“更新日期”。
